Introduction

Here you can explore some of the world's most famous composers: Georges Bizet, Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, Giacomo Puccini, Giuseppe Verdi, and Richard Wagner. Each one of these composers have shaped classical music in unique ways. Each page will dive into a different composer's life, cover their famous pieces, and highlight the lasting impact that they have had on music in culture. Whether you are a fan or newcomer, here you can discover more about these remarkable musicians!
